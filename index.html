About Me

Welcome to my webpage and thank you for visiting me here! I am a 19 year old enjoying my time at IIT Kanpur, marvelling at the next curious thing that passes by me.
I am deeply inspired by the plethora of creative ideas that humans conceive every passing day and feel proud to be living in this day and age! This turned me into that kid who identifies as an avid reader and an lover of all things literature, poetry, dance and museum-worthy or afternoon art alike.
Engineering and technology is an extension of the marvel I share with the above! Any kind of word rendition would be insufficient to express what I feel when I look at the skyscrapers on the internet, the IronMan suit, information to be stored on DNA or our lighting fast brains and their science!
Also, I'm always up for a philosophical debate, a vlogbrothers' video or anything to do with Little Women. It'd be fun for us to catch up! Who knows what weird interest intersections we could have?
